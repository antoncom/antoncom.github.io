Main Logic Rules Main Logic Rules is the groups of scenarios of the device operation you define. The scenario consists of list of rules performing consequently. Each rule has got variables and functions to operate with them. The variables may be associated with the events occurring at the controlled device. Both the events and the variables may be associated with sensors and data channels.
